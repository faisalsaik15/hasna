MISSION

Ibn Taimiyah Foundation Academy aims to produce enlightened, competitive, and well-balanced graduates committed to promoting Islamic culture and becoming proactive members of society through the acquisition of holistic knowledge, appropriate skills, and worthy attitudes and values.

VISION

Ibn Taimiyah Foundation Academy envisions being a leading Muslim private educational institution in the delivery of a quality dunya-akhirat education and a potential partner in nation-building.

GOALS

  • To role model an Islamically-oriented school administration and management.
  • To provide quality dunya-akhirat education from elementary to high school levels.
  • To develop pupils and students upholding Tawhidic principles and culture in Islam.
  • To develop and sustain capabilities of school administrators, teaching, and non-teaching personnel.
  • To deliver relevant educational programs and services to all stakeholders.
  • To establish and strengthen linkages with partners for academic and infrastructure development.
  • To foster unity and cooperation among other schools for the advancement of Bangsamoro education.
